Good centrifuge data depends as much on experimental craft as on the machine itself. This page collects the practical side of the technique:

* **Model preparation** — sand pluviation (air and water), clay consolidation from slurry, layered profiles, and sample quality control
* **In-flight actuation** — loading arms, servo-actuators and multi-axis robots; monotonic, cyclic, and storm-sequence load control
* **Instrumentation** — pore pressure transducers, LVDTs and laser sensors, strain-gauged model piles, miniature load cells, accelerometers
* **In-flight site investigation** — miniature CPT and T-bar/ball penetrometers for characterising the model ground at prototype stress levels
* **Image-based measurement** — PIV/DIC through transparent windows for full-field displacement measurement
