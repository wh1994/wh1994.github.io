A starting library for anyone who wants to learn centrifuge modelling properly:

**Textbooks & monographs**

* Taylor, R. N. (ed.) — *Geotechnical Centrifuge Technology*
* Madabhushi, G. — *Centrifuge Modelling for Civil Engineers*
* Schofield, A. N. — the Rankine Lecture "Cambridge geotechnical centrifuge operations" (Géotechnique, 1980), still essential reading

**Journals & conferences**

* *International Journal of Physical Modelling in Geotechnics* (IJPMG) — the community's dedicated journal, where I serve as Associate Editor
* The ICPMG conference series (International Conference on Physical Modelling in Geotechnics), held every four years
* ISSMGE Technical Committee TC104 (Physical Modelling in Geotechnics)

**Getting access**

* Transnational access programmes such as GEOLAB open Europe's key facilities to external researchers
