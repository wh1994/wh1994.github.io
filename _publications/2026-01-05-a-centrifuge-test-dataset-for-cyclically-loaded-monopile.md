Data article accompanying the openly available [MIDAS centrifuge dataset (TU Delft tests)](https://doi.org/10.4121/d04edde3-c05d-4818-9f64-bf1a2f3f86a6) on 4TU.ResearchData: 23 centrifuge tests (2 monotonic, 21 cyclic) on an instrumented model monopile (D = 18 mm, L/D = 5) in dense Geba sand at 100g, within the [MIDAS](/portfolio/midas/) project.
